Most Important Insights

From birth we have a natural will to strive, understand and do the right thing. Never lose it.

Knowledge is infinite, everything else is not.

Be Original. You are already differentiated by nature. Embrace your unique curiosity and stay differentiated. Similarity has no value for the world.

What matters is our actions.

Never stopping to strive (curiosity, creativity, contribution) and connect (appreciation, respect). Never get complacent.

Solve all problems that come your way. See challenges as missions we need to complete. They are the greatest gifts for us. This is when we learn and grow the most. Embrace them and look forward to them.

Question everything and think critically. Judge the world only after creating a perfect order in your own home and life.
